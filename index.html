Reinforcement Learning — Team GGN

Four challenges across reward shaping, RLHF, simulated robotics, and agent supply-chain security. Code, results, and live dashboards are linked below.

Challenge 4

Adaptive PID via Contextual Bandit

Contextual bandit (LinUCB) re-tunes PID gains for a line-following robot when its chassis changes. Violation rate drops from 76% to 21% vs fixed PID across 5 seeds × 30 chassis, with a side study on why a naive MAE reward invites the robot to idle.

Contextual bandit · LinUCB · PID control Dashboard
Challenge 3

RL in the Physical World

SAC + Hindsight Experience Replay policies for robotic grasping and reaching in MuJoCo (FetchPickAndPlace, FetchReach). Interactive 3D policy-rollout viewer plus a sim-to-real gap analysis against physical-robot constraints.

SAC · HER · MuJoCo · Sim-to-real Dashboard
Challenge 2

Taming the Language Model

RLHF loop on LFM2-350M for spatial reasoning (StepGame). Human preference pairs → reward model → PPO policy alignment, with an alternate run on Hinge-bio generation via DistilGPT2 + TRL.

RLHF · PPO · Preference modeling Dashboard
Challenge 1

Break the Agent — Reward Hacking

Reward-shaping experiments in MiniGrid MultiRoom with PPO. Demonstrates agent policy collapse when the reward signal is farmable, and a fix that rewards progress rather than activity.

PPO · MiniGrid
